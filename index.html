What I plan to do with this blog

By: Adham S. Ibrahim
Oct. 17, 2022

About Myself

I’m Adham, an Academy senior. I’ve enjoyed independent study for as long as I can remember, and have been in the GT program since last year. This year, I’m co-teaching AP Calculus BC with Mr. Bermel and bringing a new perspective to the class.

About this website

This website will be a place to gather all of the materials and work I produce this year, as well as a space where I can create posts about math topics that I think about and enjoy. I’ve chosen Idyll, a static website generator with support for interactive components using React. To be honest, I don’t know much about web development, but I’m excited to learn what it takes to produce a pretty website! If Idyll becomes too cumbersome to use or doesn’t suit my interests, I might try another platform.

Here are some of the specific things I hope to host on this website:

  1. Lesson plans. Throughout the year, I’ll be creating and teaching certain lessons, sometimes corresponding to the standard curriculum taught by Mr. Bermel, and other times with deviations. I hope to take my favorite ones and package them neatly into blog posts.
  2. Publication of the projects. Perhaps what I’m most excited to get out of this year is seeing students work on group projects that I’ve designed. I hope to post their results and document my discussions with them.
  3. Posts on miscellaneous math topics. There are so many intriguing math topics and problems out there, but they don’t get enough visibility. They’re hidden away in textbooks or lecture notes. I hope to find topics I like and write about them, with the goal of presenting these topics to people who aren’t specialized in math but want to learn more about it.
